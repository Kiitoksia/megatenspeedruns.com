## Speedrun History

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="Any% - Easy">
<Steps>
1. Synchronicity Prologue was released in Japan on October 16, 2017.
2. The first recorded speedrun was done by fatkid with a time of [**17:33**](https://youtu.be/Me7FUaRN3u4?si=A-nQVWu2x0fkkzPc).
3. On 2017/11/08, zDocRiderz claimed the world record with a time of **13:32**.
4. On 2017/12/09, otogitune beat the world record with a time of **10:03**.
5. The current world record was done on 2018/03/18 by otogitune with a time of [**9:21**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/239986838)
</Steps>
</TabItem>
