## Speedrun History
<Steps>
1. Digital Devil Story: Megami Tensei II was released in Japan on April 6, 1990.
2. The first recorded speedrun was done by akiyabo with a time of **3:46:26**.
3. On 2024/03/14, Symm achieved a new world record of [**2:53:59**](https://youtu.be/wlcupjWzV3Q)
3. The current world record is held by akiyabo on 2024/10/11 with a time of [**2:21:58**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/2273267875).
</Steps>
