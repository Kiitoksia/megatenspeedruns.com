## Speedrun History

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="Any% - GBC">
<Steps> 
1. Megami Tensei Gaiden - Last Bible was released in North America in August 1999.
2. The first ever recorded speedrun was done by futarime, uploaded on 2011/04/10, with a time of [**1:28:30**](https://www.nicovideo.jp/watch/sm14120102).
3. The first ever recorded speedrun of the English version, Revelations: The Demon Slayer, was done by WardenOfSamsara on 2016/11/10 with a time of [**1:52:57**](https://youtu.be/ym29EqjneOA)
4. The current world record is held by sioneus, done on 2020/11/30, with a time of [**1:27:30**](https://youtu.be/gGqGqoAcE0U)
</Steps>
</TabItem>
