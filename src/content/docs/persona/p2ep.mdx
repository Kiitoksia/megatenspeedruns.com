## Speedrun History
<Tabs>
    <TabItem label="PS1">
        <Steps>
            1. Persona 2: Eternal Punishment was released in Japan on June 29, 2000.
            2. The first recorded run was done by kosamestl on 2020/01/03 with a time of [**6:04:51**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/527898436). They improved the time down to a time of [**5:54:27**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/616672790)
            3. On 2020/08/13, mizusima40 beat the world record with a time of [**5:44:25**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/713875415).
            4. On 2020/11/29, kosamestl reclaimed the world record with a time of [**5:38:22**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/819598386)
            5. The current world record holder is mizusima40 with a time of [**5:29:44**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/884576403)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem label="PSP">
        <Steps>
            1. Persona 2: Eternal Punishment was released in Japan on May 17, 2012.
            2. The first recorded run was done by ToxicTT on 2021/07/24 with a time of [**4:39:23**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aroBOF6NEP0)
            3. The current world record holder is ToxicTT on 2022/10/06 with a time of [**2:52:41**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZT4EUxQrdDQ)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Categories
<CardGrid>
    <Card title="Any%">Main category, beat the game as fast as possible using either Nanjo (Nate) or Eriko (Elly) in the mid-game.</Card>
    <Card title="EX">Category where you beat the game with both available partners (Nanjo and Eriko) back-to-back, and then beat the EX dungeon with game's ultimate boss.</Card>
</CardGrid>
