## Speedrun History
<Tabs>
    <TabItem label="ノーマル - Any%">
        <Steps>
            1. Shin Megami Tensei: Nocturne was released in Japan on February 20, 2003.
            2. The first available recorded run was done by PinkPajamas with a time of [**8:49:35**](https://secure.twitch.tv/pinkpajamas/v/78386098). He'd keep the record and improve it to a time of [**7:29:54**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/221672763)
            3. 3 years later, ShinerCCC would claim the world record and achieve a time of [**7:28:41**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/926292393) which he brought down to [**7:07:46**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1023318379)
            4. The current world record is held by Jonperi23 with a time of [**6:56:45**](https://youtu.be/L1V4qZILLUM)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem label="ハード - True Demon Ending">
        <Steps>
        1. Shin Megami Tensei: Nocturne was released in Japan on February 20, 2003.
        2. The first ever recorded available time was done by tobira on 2012/05/06 with a time of [**15:35:33**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/44841268)
        3. On 2016/06/19, PinkPajamas first ran his own route and achieved a world record with a time of [**15:26:29**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/73284508) which he improved a week later to a time of [**13:46:02**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/97952335)
        4. On 2017/01/15, mizusima40 claimed the record with a time of [**13:20:19**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B4KCZvbOlmK2ckVEb2ZWRE5qdHc/view)
        5. 2 months later, PinkPajamas reclaimed the record with a time of [**13:11:19**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/134465834)
        6. 2 months later, *again*, mizusima40 dramatically claimed the record with a time of [**12:50:29**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B4KCZvbOlmK2dFpZT1NiOUtZbXc/edit)
        7. PinkPajamas fought long and hard and 9 months later reclaimed the record with a time of [**12:40:18**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/250716238)
        8. On 2018/05/19, mizusima40 achieved a record that would now stand for over a year, a time of [**12:13:42**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/263271085). On 2022/08/20, he'd improve it to a time of [**11:15:44**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1567450872)
        9. On a warm, sunny day of May, Jonperi23 was the first person to steal the world record from mizusima in over 6 years and claimed the first sub 11h time with his time of [**10:58:32**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/2153786416)
        10. 16 days later, in a dramatic turn of events, mizusima40 conquered the world record with a time of [**10:49:55**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/2167663903)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
</Tabs>
